# RugPull Tokenomics

### **1. Token Supply Allocation**

The total supply of **RugPull Token** is distributed across several categories to ensure long-term sustainability, community growth, and a healthy ecosystem economy.

***

### **1.1 Community — 300,000,000**

This allocation fuels user incentives, network participation, and large-scale community programs.

#### **• Future Incentives — 170,000,000**

Reserved for future reward programs, including:

* Retroactive programs for early contributors
* Creator and developer incentives
* Community growth campaigns
* Tooling and ecosystem support initiatives

#### **• Router Rewards — 30,000,000**

An initial reward pool to support early router operators and infrastructure providers.\
Its purpose is to:

* Encourage router participation
* Improve bandwidth routes and network stability
* Sustain infrastructure until network fees naturally support operations

#### **• Airdrop One — 100,000,000**

A large initial airdrop designed to broaden distribution and reward early community members, helping seed the first wave of ecosystem participants.

***

### **1.2 Foundation & Ecosystem Growth — 228,000,000**

Managed by the RugPull Foundation, this allocation supports the expansion and long-term health of the network:

* Operational and administrative costs
* Research and development
* Protocol upgrades and maintenance
* Partnerships and integrations
* Community initiatives and marketing

Usage of these tokens will eventually be governed by the RugPull DAO to ensure transparency and decentralization.

***

### **1.3 Early Investors — 252,000,000**

Allocated to early supporters of the project under a vesting schedule:

* **1-year cliff**
* **1-year vesting**

Tokens remain locked and **cannot be staked** until fully vested.

***

### **1.4 Contributors — 220,000,000**

This category covers the core team, developers, and future contributors.

Vesting schedule:

* **1-year cliff**
* **3-year vesting**

Locked tokens are non-transferable and cannot participate in staking until vested.

***

### **2. Monthly Distribution Schedule**

RugPull Token follows a structured monthly release schedule to ensure:

* Predictable supply introduction
* Reduced volatility
* Long-term alignment between the community, investors, and contributors

A detailed distribution chart will be published once the RugPull DAO governance framework is active.
